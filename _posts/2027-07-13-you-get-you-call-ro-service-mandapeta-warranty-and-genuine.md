<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Professional TDS Controller Service</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
A frequently overlooked component of RO systems is the [TDS controller service](https://www.rocareindia.com/ro-water-purifier-service-east-godawari). The TDS controller is a valve that regulates the mineral content of your purified water by adjusting the Total Dissolved Solids level . Professional service ensures this critical component functions correctly.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>What the TDS controller does:</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The TDS controller enables customers to retain natural minerals in purified water according to their requirements . Turning the screw of the valve anticlockwise increases mineral content, while turning it clockwise decreases mineral content . Professional technicians recommend keeping TDS at the lowest possible level but not below 50 mg/L .</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
